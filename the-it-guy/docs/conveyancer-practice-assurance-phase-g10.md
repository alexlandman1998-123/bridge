# Conveyancer practice assurance and controlled pilot — Phase G10

G10 certifies G1–G9 as one attorney operating system. Every phase supplies one immutable checkpoint bound to the same release, build, commit, environment, organisation, firm and firm-policy fingerprint. Checkpoints require the exact phase version, full scenario success, no skips, immutable evidence, every cross-phase safety probe and independent manager review.

Certification requires all thirteen agreed scenarios: individual cash and financed purchasers, existing-bond cancellation, company and trust parties, enhanced due diligence, third-party payments, changed beneficiary details, restricted matters, attorney replacement, historical import, provider failure with manual fallback and closed-matter reopening.

Concrete G8 portal-isolation evidence and G9 migration/recovery evidence are mandatory. Compliance, legal, trust, privacy and operations sign-offs must be performed by different people.

The pilot defaults to 100% expected outcomes and zero critical, authority, access, FICA, trust, privacy, integrity, migration, recovery or side-effect failures. It is limited to three firms and 25 matters, requires named assurance/support/rollback ownership and a kill switch, and does not require external providers.

G10 is read-only certification. It cannot grant portal access, approve evidence, move trust money, send correspondence, import data, replay webhooks, restore databases, roll back or deploy. Durable checkpoint storage, telemetry and deployment approval remain productisation responsibilities.
