# Conveyancer Matter Exceptions — Phase B7

## Purpose

Phase B7 closes the initial exception programme with deterministic assurance evidence and a guarded pilot contract spanning B1 through B6.

The executable service is `src/services/attorneyWorkflow/conveyancerMatterExceptionAssurance.js`.

## Per-matter assurance

Critical platform checks certify:

- The active A-series matter plan and exact plan/transaction/organisation bindings.
- B1 exception contract validity.
- B2 library integrity and definition provenance.
- B3 active-scope uniqueness.
- B4 correction and not-applicable review coherence.
- B5 accepted-risk waiver governance.
- B6 override scope, duration, safeguards and manager authority.
- Complete activation and mutation audit chains, runtime revisions, actor authority and independent decisions.

Matter-health checks observe active exceptions, unresolved critical exceptions, accepted-risk waivers, active or expired overrides and breached resolution SLAs. These conditions need operational attention but do not imply that the exception platform is defective.

Critical platform failures produce `blocked`; matter-health concerns produce `observe`; clean evidence produces `ready`. A frozen evidence packet can be serialized for release and pilot records.

## Pilot suite

The default suite executes the real B1-B6 services across:

- Explicit activation from an observed signal.
- Evidence-backed correction and independent review.
- A governed not-applicable decision.
- An independent accepted-risk waiver.
- A temporary manager-approved override.
- Critical override authority, including denial of non-manager approval and unsafe operations.

Unexpected workflow outcomes hold the pilot. Operational thresholds cover activation failures, audit gaps, accepted unauthorised commands, command failure rate, unresolved critical rate and expired override rate.

## Pilot controls

- Expansion decisions are `go`, `observe` or `hold`.
- Named assurance, rollback and support owners are mandatory.
- Legacy exception handling and a kill switch remain available.
- Automatic resolution, waiver approval and override approval remain disabled.
- The manifest cannot enable database writes.

## Phase boundary

B7 produces assurance, pilot and release evidence only. It does not persist exceptions or events, enrol firms, activate feature flags, approve decisions, clean up expired overrides or execute rollback actions.
