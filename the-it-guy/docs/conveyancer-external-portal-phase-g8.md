# Conveyancer client and professional portal — Phase G8

G8 defines controlled external participation without granting internal matter access. Invitations and grants are exact-firm, exact-matter, expiring and reference-only. Identity validation and affirmative consent are mandatory.

Clients see only `client_visible` status, requests, uploads, appointments, messages and acknowledgements. External professionals may additionally see explicitly `professional_shared` records. Client-risk assessments, compliance escalations, privileged notes, trust controls, internal exceptions, staff performance and profitability are always prohibited.

Uploads must pass malware scanning and remain evidence proposals pending internal review. Comments, replies, acknowledgements and preference changes remain pending internal review. Downloads require explicit permission and watermarking. Revocation and professional replacement are immutable intents rather than silent access changes.

No raw invite token, identity challenge, internal record content or privileged material is exposed by this contract. Persistence, invitation delivery, token verification, storage, portal screens and database RLS remain productisation responsibilities.
